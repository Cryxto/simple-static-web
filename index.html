"Affordable Big Bike"

Kawasaki

Author's Thought

  Kawasaki ER6n specifications are fairly impressive in its class. This motor is capable of providing extraordinary torque. The design also has the feel of a big motorbike or big motorbike because of its dense and full shape. Including the use of wide legs. The impression of being dense and filled is inseparable from its fairly heavy weight. Besides that, the use of a tubular frame combined with the placement of a unique rear shockbreaker really has a strong BIG BIKE feel. So it's no wonder that many are captivated by the Kawasaki ER6n with these specifications.

  According to KJV Motorsport, this bike price is really affordable than other big bike (above 250cc). The price is arround 60 million to 70 million Rupiah (Rp 60.000.000 - Rp 80.000.000). So that's the reason why people wanted this bike eventhough there is no new unit (production of this bike and its variant ER6f stopped at 2018).
